About me

Github LinkedIn Email

I am a developer who loves all things programming and Linux. I am passionate about open-source software, electronics and automation. On this website you can find out more about me and what I do, as well as a blog where I sometimes write about some of the more interesting things that I’m working on.

I am currently in my final year of studying Computer Engineering at the University of Pretoria, where I’m also working as the team-lead for the development and systems administration team for the Department of Computer Science.

I enjoy working on challenging programming and electronics projects and I’m hoping to start my career working in intelligent robotics when I graduate. My interests include machine learning, electronic engineering and all things Linux. I have a keen interest in developing software using best-practices and I’m hoping that by opening it up to more scrutiny I will learn more in the process. I’m also a firm believer in the open-source philosophy and try to open-source as many of my projects as I can.

In my free time I enjoy learning more about topics that interest me and occasionally binge-watching a series in an unhealthily short time. I also maintain a homelab where I get to try out the new things that I learn and deploy services that I often use.

Feel free to get in touch with me using any of the platforms listed on this website, I’d love to hear your feedback.
