Hi, my name is Kobus

I am a passionate engineer who loves working on systems, algorithms, and the finer details of how software interacts with hardware. On this website you can find out more about what I do, as well as a blog where I sometimes write about the technical concepts I’m exploring.

I enjoy working on challenging programming projects, particularly those where efficiency and speed are critical. My interests include high-performance computing, low-level optimization, and understanding the Linux kernel. I’m a keen Linux user and a firm believer in the open-source philosophy.

In my free time I enjoy working on various projects with the aim of always learning something new.

Feel free to get in touch with me using any of the platforms listed on this website, I’d love to hear your feedback.
